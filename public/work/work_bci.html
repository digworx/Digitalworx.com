Basic Concepts Inc.

Website    /    Design & Development    /    Ecommerce    /    Wordpress

Basic Concepts Inc. (BCI), a manufacturer of industrial spill containment equipment, hired Digitalworx to build their new ecommerce website from the ground up. They needed a product driven site thate would easily and quickly communicate technical details about each product across multiple targeted customers—industrial, government, and military.

Using Wordpress as the CMS for this custom built site, we used the WooCommerce as the backbone of the ecommerce functionality, and configured it's many excellent plugins to create the site and fulfill BCI's exact requirements.
