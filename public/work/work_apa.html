American Planning Assocation (APA)

Website    /    Design    /    Wordpress

The American Planning Association hired Digitalworx to design a new Wordpress-based website for their Plan4Health initiative. Plan4Health is a collaboration between several organizations promoting public health and public planning.

The new site was designed for a custom Wordpress theme and it includes a full featured blog, video, and RSS feeds. The talented APA web development team took our wireframes, prototypes, and designs and internally developed and deployed the site.
