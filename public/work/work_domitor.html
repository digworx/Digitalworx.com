Domitor, the international society for the study of early cinema, is an association for people with a scholarly interest in cinema from its beginnings to 1915. We've worked closely with Domitor since 2008, building two generations of websites and designing promotional materials for their biannual conferences.

Our most recent project was to build an entirely new website for them. The new site was custom designed and custom themed in Wordpress. It is a dual language (English/Spanish) site with a blog, membership management, discussion groups, and private member areas. Translation between English/Spanish is handled by the WPML Wordpress plugin.

This site was selected as a Nominee for the Awwwards Site of the Day as well as for CSS Nectar's Site of the Day.
